Because I am passionate about designing products that encourage digital exploration.


A Digital Cover Letter by Cassandra Villegas

I am a digital and interactive designer interested in everything related to branding and web design. I’m especially interested in the Product Designer position because I think I would be a great fit based on the responsibilities and qualifications. I would love for the chance to apply for the open position and give you a sense of the type of designer I am.

As an introduction to myself, I’ve worked on a variety of projects, from web and app design, to branding and print design. Currently, I am the lead designer for Portico, a travel inspiration and planning web application. My position involves creating wireframes for user testing, designing static mockups for web application development, and assisting with front end development for the web application. I have also been leading the UX and UI design for our mobile application that we are currently building for a first round of user tests. The Portico office is located in Boston, MA, however I work remotely from Kingston, NY and have been comfortably working remotely with the team for nearly three years.

Before I started my position with Portico, I was a Project Manager and Designer for Medical Bound, a marketing agency located out of New York, NY. My role involved managing and designing a variety of projects, from landing pages and websites, to brochures and business cards. I worked with an entirely remote team of six and handled all communication with our clients throughout the project process.

I’m very passionate about creating enjoyable digital experiences for users, and am always up for a challenge. I would love to work with GitHub and I think I could bring a lot to the team.

Thank you so much and I hope we can work together in the future!
