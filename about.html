Hi There!


I am a fifth year honors student at Northeastern University in the College of Arts, Media, and Design, concentrating in Graphic and Information Design and minoring in Art History and Architectural History.

During my time at Northeastern, I have learned a great deal about design both in the classroom and during my free time. I recently finished my digital design co-op at America's Test Kitchen, where I worked on web and UX design, and am now a freelance designer for the same company. Over the past year, I have also been a part of SCOUT, Northeastern’s student-lead design studio. In the spring and summer of 2015, I acted as the Creative Lead for IDEA, Northeastern University's Venture Accelerator. I have also done freelance work for several organizations and companies, including Peoples Television Inc., Northeastern University’s Center for Resilience Studies, and Northeastern University's Global Student Success Office.

I am an enthusiastic, hard-working, and dedicated designer who is always looking to learn more about design.
