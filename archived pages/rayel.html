Project Details

As my first project through Scout, Northeastern’s student-lead design studio, I worked with two other designers on an identity redesign project for a local musician. The artist RAYEL was looking for an updated logo and identity system that could be used on album covers, stickers, and other paraphernalia. Our team came up with three logo directions that focused on her Boston origins and interest in Buddhism. We landed on the final logo, which features a lotus flower that acts as the “Y” in RAYEl.
